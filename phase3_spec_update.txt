## Section to ADD or UPDATE: Pricing System

### Price Tier System (FINAL - confirmed 2026-04-28)

Pricing is based on cumulative claimed blocks across all stages (new purchases only, renewals do not count).

| Cumulative Claimed Blocks | Full Stage Monthly | Per Block Price |
|---|---|---|
| 0 - 20,000 | $1,000/mo | $0.40/block |
| 20,001 - 50,000 | $2,000/mo | $0.70/block |
| 50,001 - 90,000 | $3,500/mo | $1.10/block |
| 90,001 - 140,000 | $5,000/mo | $1.60/block |
| 140,001 - 300,000 | $7,500/mo | $2.40/block |
| 300,001 - 800,000 | $10,000/mo | $3.10/block |
| 800,001+ | $12,500/mo | $4.00/block |

**Price lock rule:** Buyer keeps their purchase price as long as subscription is active. Cancellation loses the locked price.

**Annual plan:** 10 months upfront (2 months free). Applies to full stage purchases too.

**Minimum purchase:** $1 or more. No minimum block count.

**Volume discounts (apply to per-block purchases only, NOT full stage):**
- 200-499 blocks: -10%
- 500-999 blocks: -20%
- 1,000+ blocks: -30%

**Full stage monopoly rules:**
- Buys entire stage at current full-stage price
- No volume discount applied
- Annual discount applies
- If some blocks already owned by others, monopoly buyer does NOT immediately take them
- When existing owners cancel, their blocks automatically transfer to the monopoly buyer
- Full stage price does NOT change due to existing owned blocks

**Maximum ownership:** 18,432 blocks per buyer (2 full stages = 2 × 9,216)

### Permanent Purchase (Lifetime)

- Unlocks when full-stage monthly price reaches $7,500
- Price = buyer's current monthly price × 24 months
- Cap: $10,000/mo and above → flat $240,000
- Full-stage monopoly buyers only
- One-time upfront, no recurring payments
- No cancellation or refund after purchase

### Price Progression (UI)

- Publicly presented as "grid filling up" — do NOT explain as revenue-based internally
- Progress bar shows claimed blocks vs next tier trigger
- "Prices rise as the world fills" messaging
- Early buyers lock in their price

### Grid Specifications (CONFIRMED)

- All stages: 128 × 72 = 9,216 blocks per stage
- No creator zones, no sub-divided stages — all stages identical grid size
- 1 block = 1 color, fixed
- Retro mode: visual pixelation to 80×45 appearance only, grid data unchanged
