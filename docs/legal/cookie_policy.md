## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how the site is used.

neverEND also uses similar technologies such as local storage and session storage for authentication purposes.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |

## 3. Cookie Consent

### 3.1 EU / UK Users

If you are located in the EU, EEA, or UK, we will present you with a cookie consent banner on your first visit. You can:

- **Accept all cookies**
- **Reject non-essential cookies**
- **Manage preferences** individually

Essential cookies cannot be rejected as they are required for the Service to function.

### 3.2 Other Users

For users outside the EU/EEA/UK, analytics and preference cookies may be enabled by default where permitted by applicable law. Where required, consent will be obtained.

## 4. Managing Cookies

### 4.1 Cookie Preference Center

You can update your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the website footer.

### 4.2 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent the Service from functioning correctly.

### 4.3 Google Analytics Opt-Out

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on (tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
