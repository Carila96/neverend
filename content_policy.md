# Grid Placement & Content Policy

**neverEND** — operated by Carila
Last updated: April 2025

This Policy forms part of the Terms of Service and governs the purchase, use, and moderation of Grid Placements within neverEND.

---

## 1. Placement Categories

### 1.1 Standard Commercial Placements

Available on all non-free stages. Single color per block, selected via color picker. Suitable for brand colors, simple logos, and solid-fill designs.

### 1.2 Creator Zone Placements

Available on every 5th stage (Stages 5, 10, 15, 20, etc.). PNG image upload permitted. Suitable for pixel art, detailed illustrations, and multi-color designs.

### 1.3 Enterprise Custom Placements

Available upon request for large-scale or exclusive arrangements. May include priority placement, dedicated stage sections, custom review SLAs, and negotiated pricing outside the standard tier. Contact [enterprise email] for details.

---

## 2. Content Specifications

| Zone | Format | Size | Colors |
|---|---|---|---|
| Standard | Color picker (hex) | 1 block = 16×16px | Single color per block |
| Creator | PNG upload | Up to purchased block area | Full color |

- Maximum file size for PNG uploads: **2MB**
- Animated content: **not supported** at this time
- Embedded links or QR codes: **not permitted** within the grid
- Text within designs: permitted if legible at 16px resolution
- Content must be designed at or near 16px pixel resolution for best results

## 3. Brand Safety Standards

To maintain a brand-safe environment for all purchasers, the following content categories are subject to additional review or are prohibited:

### 3.1 Prohibited Sectors

The following industries may not advertise through Grid Placements without prior written approval from Carila:

- Adult entertainment
- Gambling and betting services
- Tobacco and nicotine products
- Weapons and firearms retailers
- Unregulated financial products (including certain crypto assets)
- Political campaign advertising

### 3.2 Content Standards

All placements must comply with the following:

- **No misleading claims:** Content must not make false or unsubstantiated claims about products or services
- **No comparative claims:** Direct negative comparisons to named competitors are not permitted
- **No hate or discriminatory content:** As defined in the Terms of Service Section 5.3
- **No deceptive design:** Content must not impersonate another brand or create false associations

### 3.3 Advertiser Disclosure

Grid Placements of a commercial or promotional nature will be identifiable as purchaser-placed content within the game's interface. Carila does not editorially endorse any purchaser's products or services.

## 4. Review Process & SLA

### 4.1 Standard Review

- Submissions are reviewed within **2 business days** of upload
- Review includes automated screening followed by human verification
- Automated tools may flag content for human review but will not make final decisions on placement affecting paid subscriptions

### 4.2 Priority Review (Business & Enterprise)

- Business plan subscribers: review within **1 business day**
- Enterprise plan subscribers: review SLA negotiated in separate agreement

### 4.3 Review Outcomes

- **Approved:** Content goes live within 24 hours of approval
- **Rejected:** Purchaser notified by email with general reason; resubmission permitted
- **Flagged for clarification:** Carila may request additional information before making a decision

---

## 5. Cure Process & Removal

### 5.1 Curable Violations

For minor policy violations (e.g., content slightly exceeding specifications, minor brand standard issues), Carila will:

1. Notify the purchaser by email with a description of the issue
2. Provide a **72-hour correction window**
3. Suspend (not remove) the placement during the correction window
4. Restore the placement upon receipt of compliant content

### 5.2 Severe Violations

The following violations may result in **immediate removal without prior notice**:

- Illegal content of any kind
- Child safety risks
- Clear and unambiguous IP infringement (e.g., exact reproduction of a registered trademark without authorization)
- Malware, phishing, or fraud
- Hate speech or content promoting violence against identifiable groups

### 5.3 Dispute & Appeal

If you believe your content was incorrectly removed or rejected, you may submit an appeal to [IP email] within **14 days** of the removal notice. Carila will review appeals and respond within **5 business days**.

### 7.3 Fan Art Policy

Carila is not responsible for visual combinations that may result from adjacent blocks purchased by different parties. Each purchaser is responsible only for their own block(s).

Where adjacent placements create a clear brand safety issue, risk of confusion, or prohibited visual combination, **Carila may, at its discretion, relocate, mask, suspend, or otherwise remediate the affected placement arrangement**. Affected purchasers will be notified and offered a cure opportunity where feasible.

## 8. Grid Persistence & Post-Cancellation

- Upon subscription cancellation, your content remains displayed until the end of the current billing period
- After the billing period ends, your blocks will be replaced with a neutral placeholder (grey)
- Content published in third-party game recordings, streams, or videos prior to cancellation may persist indefinitely as Carila cannot retroactively edit third-party content
- This persistence is acknowledged and accepted by all purchasers at the time of purchase
